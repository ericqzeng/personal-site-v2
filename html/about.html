Eric is a junior at The Wharton School majoring in Computer Science and Business Analytics. Originally from San Jose, California, he is currently in the Philadelphia region.

As both a software developer and photographer, Eric seeks to find ways to constantly learn, grow, and create. He currently will be working for Captial One over the summer in New York City but is always open to exploring new industries and places. As an avid fan of all things engineering, Eric has a fond interest in all things "techy", ranging from software development, computer electronics, consumer devices, and even motorsports (he will talk to you all day about racing).

Photography has been one of Eric's keen interests. Since picking up the camera in 2016, Eric has gone on to photograph events around the Philadelphia region. He is a staff member of The Daily Pennsylvanian, UPenn's student-run newspaper. Feel free to contact him for any inquries regarding his work on this site.

At UPenn, Eric is also a dancer with Pan-Asian Dance Troupe, which holds an annual performance celebrating Asian cultures through dance. As a current Production Chair and former Vice President of External Relations and Treasurer, he has experience in both performing and managing a dance troupe. He enjoys spending time with his troupe mates while ensuring the group can perform at its highest level.
